How can we help?

Our goal is to develop the first AI-powered platform designed specifically for chronic hyperventilation (combined with other conditions, like anxiety and digestive issues). We will work closely together with health professionals to obtain this goal.

Instead of generic breathing apps that don't understand your unique situation, we intend to use AI to identify your patterns, help you recognize triggers, and recommend personalized treatments based on what's worked for other people in your situation.

Track symptoms in seconds. See patterns you never noticed. Get insights that finally make sense of what you're experiencing.

Built by someone who's lived it.

Hi, I'm Thomas. I've dealt with long periods of constant air hunger and forced yawning for almost 20 years. I saw countless doctors, tried 7 different anti-depressants, had dozens of therapy sessions, and tried multiple breathing techniques. Some things helped, others didn't. No one seemed to really understand the problem, and it felt like an endless cycle in which I would eventually end up alone.

I realized I had tremendous difficulty recognizing triggers and patterns, especially because thinking clearly felt nearly impossible. I needed help with this on a daily basis. But there was no tool on the market that could provide the support that I needed. So I decided to build one.

This tool, combined with the right medical and therapeutic care, will provide much quicker insight into your struggle and how to overcome it.

I need your help to build this right. Your 5 minutes could help thousands of people like us finally get the jump start we've needed all along.
